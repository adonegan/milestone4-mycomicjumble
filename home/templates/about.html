About My Comic Jumble

When I was 12 years old I started collecting comics - a lot of comics. I loved Marvel, DC and anything I could get my hands on. Thus, my love affair with comics began. When you've finished it, feel free to pop back over to My Comic Jumble to let me know your thoughts!
