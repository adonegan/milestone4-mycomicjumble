Frequently Asked Questions

Do you really own all of these comics?

Yes, I started collecting them when I was kid and just never stopped.

Why are you selling them and why now?

Well, a lot of people ask me this. I'm starting a family and need to make room in my house.

How come you don't just give them away?

I've spent years collecting and maintaining my comics. I want them to go to a good home with someone who appreciates them as much as me!

Why will happen with your website after you sell everything?

I have a finite amount of comics, yes. When everything's sold I'll probably keep this website running, but not as a shop - I'll likely turn it into a forum so those who bought the comics can keep in touch.

Can I return a comic if I change my mind?

No, all sales are final. You can, however, pass them on to someone else who would enjoy them.
